This design is a tribute to the cartoons that shaped my youth and still bring me
joy today. A nostalgic collection of cartoons that defined my childhood, from the
characters I watched on Saturday mornings to the ones I still rewatch in the
present time. I still remember watching Sofia the First at 7:30am whenever
school was cancelled. I cherish the memories where my kuya and I sat on the
couch and fought over which cartoons to watch. This piece brings together my
favorite characters from cartoons like Gravity Falls, Teen Titans Go, and Phineas
and Ferb, celebrating the cartoons I grew up with and still love to watch.
