MY TOP READS

Since 2020, my world has been a vibrant tapestry of manhwa, manga, and manhua, each thread woven with personal memories. Some stories were just for fun, my little guilty pleasures, while others have been constant companions, their long-running plots marking the passage of time. I've shared countless laughs and wild theories with friends, turning fictional plots into real-life memories we still talk about. There were those series I checked on every single day, waiting for a new chapter with an almost breathless anticipation. A few of them have even altered my brain chemistry, shaping how I think and feel, and some have offered truly valuable life lessons I'll carry with me. Every one of these stories, whether deeply profound or just a simple escape, holds a special place in my heart.
