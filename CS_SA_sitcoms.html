Sitcom is an abbreviation for "situation comedy." It is a comical TV show with a realistic
setting in which the same cast of individuals are involved in a variety of amusing
scenarios. For example, consider an apartment with hilarious roommates or a restaurant
with a consistent group of characters cracking jokes week after week.
