I have rewatched this comedy so many times that I've lost count. In the past, it was a comfort show for me; I would have it playing in the background, and it helped me not have any negative thoughts. My favorite character is Kokomi Teruhashi. I also love the fast-paced, witty humor and the constant running gags. The show is great at balancing all the eccentric characters with Saiki’s sarcastic commentary, which makes it endlessly entertaining. however, i feel that if this show was not a comedy, it would be absolutely tragic and heart-breaking due to the understated issues and plot behind the jokes.
This anime holds a lot of sentimental value for me, because I've rewatched the whole thing 8 times. The animation is great, and I love the character designs and the detailed world-building. As an isekai, I enjoy watching the main character build up this new world from nothing. I also love how the show manages to be a power fantasy without the main character being an abusive tyrant; instead, they focus on building a community where different species can coexist. It’s a great blend of slice-of-life and high-stakes conflict, with a lot of satisfying character evolutions along the way.

In my opinion, this anime is peak and the best of the best. It is a full 367-episode show. It checks all the boxes: Layered protagonist, The whole antagonistic cast have their own detailed backstory, Complex tragic villain (I personally love how their main purpose isn’t to defeat the hero, but to build their character.), Best plot twists, A balance of comedy and soul-crushing arcs, Found Family trope, Realistic character dynamics and relationships, Monologues, Life lessons (Themes and Message), Immersive voice acting, Smooth animation, Consistency, Superb pacing, World building, Character design, Character introductions, Personal impact. It is the full package.
